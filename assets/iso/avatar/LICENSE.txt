Avatar sprite in this directory is baked (rendered to flat PNG at a fixed
isometric camera angle, mid-stride of a walk animation) from 3D assets in
"KayKit: Adventurers Character Pack (2.0)" by Kay Lousberg
(www.kaylousberg.com), released under CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain).
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

No attribution required by the license, but noted here for provenance.

Source: tools/sprite-bake/models/rogue/Rogue.glb (character mesh +
skeleton) posed using the "Walking_A" clip from
tools/sprite-bake/models/anim/Rig_Medium_MovementBasic.glb (separately
authored animation sharing the same "Rig_Medium" bone names — Three.js
resolves the clip onto the character's skeleton by name, the standard
glTF retargeting technique). Baked via tools/sprite-bake — see that tool
to re-render at a different pose or with a different clip/character.
