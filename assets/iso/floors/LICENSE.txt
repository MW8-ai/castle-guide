Floor textures in this directory are derived from "Isometric Floor Tiles"
by Screaming Brain Studios, released under CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain).
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

Each PNG here is a small cropped/re-exported texture swatch taken from the
center of one tile in the original composite tilesheet (magenta chroma-key
corners removed by cropping), intended to be used as a repeating
ctx.createPattern() fill rather than the original diamond-tile sprite.

No attribution required by the license, but noted here for provenance.
